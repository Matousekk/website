Welcome to Used Cars-UK

Used Cars-UK are a specialist used car dealer based in Broxbourne in Hertfordshire. We are proud to offer you a first class customer service with very competitive pricing.

At Used Cars-UK we stock a range of used cars to suit all budgets and lifestyles.

We are sure to have the right car for you.

All our vehicles are carefully selected, test driven and mechanically tested to provide our customers with the best assurance in purchasing their new car.

For your own piece of mind, we also include warranties with all our vehicles which are priced over £1000. Extended warranty packages are also available. We use the UK’s leading insurance warranty specialist Auto Protect.

In addition to this, we offer finance on our vehicles. For more information do not hesitate to contact us.

We update our stock daily!

If the car you are looking for does not appear on our website, we may have it in our preparation workshop or we may be able to source it for you. Get in contact with our team today!

We strive on having a great reputation and being trustworthy. Furthermore, we offer excellent advice and experience on vehicles and maintenance. Our friendly staff will go the extra mile to guarantee that you receive the advice you need. This is the reason why many of our customers keep coming back to us.

Thinking of selling your car? One of our friendly advisors will be happy to help. Get in touch today to get a quote.

We part exchange any vehicle and offer the best price guaranteed.

Our newly refurbished office now offers facilities for customers to tax and insure their new vehicles.

At Used Cars-UK we don’t just sell cars, we do everything possible to get you driving your perfect car.

Please contact us to help you with any questions you may have, our cars are at unbeatable prices and sell very quickly.

We look forward to meeting you!
